philosophy

Identity is a cage.
We removed it.

Every social platform sells your identity back to you as a product. Your follower count, your profile, your history — they are the leash.

CipherFlux removes the leash. What you say is evaluated on its own terms — not filtered through who you are or what you said before.

Anonymity is not cowardice. It is arriving without prejudice. The idea stands alone, or it does not stand.

security & trust

No identity to expose.

The most secure system is one that never collects what it cannot protect.
